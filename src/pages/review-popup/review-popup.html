Maverick’s Awareness-Advocacy-Action review or the 3A review is an assessment of qualitative changes in terms of children’s awareness to their body, their actions to choose healthy options, their accountability to stay fit and many more which are most crucial for a lifetime committment to be fit! These are conducted in schools following the Maverick Physical Literacy Curriculum.

If you are seeing this message, either your school is not enrolled in our program or the assessment results are not yet available.
