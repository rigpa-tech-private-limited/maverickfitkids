Physical Literacy is being aware of our embodied nature and using that awareness to realise the sanctity and dignity of our body. We can choose to be physically literate by


* cultivating the desire and motivation to value our body as the epitome of life


* building our confidence and physical competence in a wide range of activities, and


* developing our knowledge and understanding to take responsibility for life.


Maverick Minute is a tool to create that awareness. Take one minute out of every hour to stay in touch with your body through the guided activities presented in these videos.
