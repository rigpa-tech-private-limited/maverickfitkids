Credentials

Maverick Fit Kids is India’s first physical literacy informed PE curriculum in schools since 2014.

Maverick is the exclusive implementation partner of the Sports, Physical Education, Fitness & Leisure Council (SPEFL-SC) for schools in Tamil Nadu & Puducherry. SPEFL-SC is the Nodal entity for promoting physical education in the country formed by the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE).

Maverick is currently the first and only private entity to represent the Government of India in presenting a structured physical education program in schools through this Government-Private partnership.

The curriculum of Maverick has been designed as a cross curricular multi-dimensional approach to physical education - not limiting it to only competitive sports. The program draws strength from the physical, affective & cognitive demands placed by classical Indian dance, team building & sportsmanship from exposure to varied sports, knowledge & understanding of health from fitness, and spiritual rootedness to value & treat the body with dignity from yoga.

Every Maverick session is designed by Gita Krishna Raj, CEO & Managing Director, Maverick, a fitness entrepreneur who combines her academic background of a Global MBA and international fitness certifications, with her creative expressions evolved as a renowned Indian classical dancer, powerful orator, spiritualist writer, inspiring actor, international award winning director. Gita is a curator of physical literacy and India Ambassador for the International physical Literacy Association (IPLA) UK.
