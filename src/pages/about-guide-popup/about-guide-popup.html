The exercises are grouped into SEVEN major types:

  • Aerobic Activity
  • Back Conditioning
  • Core conditioning
  • Upper body strength
  • Lower body strength
  • Full body work
  • Dynamic Stability

You will also find guides to Open Air gym exercises, Brain gym activities & Yoga asanas.


Several exercise may fall under more than one category. The grouping presented here is merely for convenience of selection during a circuit.


Every exercise needs different implements - Aerobic steppers, swiss ball, dumbbells, aerobic rods etc. You will also find guides for Yoga Asanas, Pranayama and Brain Gym as taught in Maverick.


Watch these animated Guides to each of these exercises to ensure you learn them right.


CREATE YOUR OWN WORKOUT @ HOME:

Use these as references to make your own circuit at home. Simply pick one exercise from each type, use home implements if you do not have fitness implements and perform each exercise as per the guidelines given.


Recommended duration:

Warm up 5 to 10 minutes

Circuit with 7 exercises with a rest of 30 to 60 seconds between exercises and a total of 3 sets - 20 minutes

Cool down 5 to 10 minutes
